Lexica Reader is a fast and accurate English essay analyzer, supplied with training data from accredited sources of essay-writing literature. It analyzes each essay using the following metrics:

Aural Cadence

Analyzes how the essay flows and feels at a physical, sound-based level, and shows how easily your essay rolls off the tongue.

Contextual Harmony

Provides insight to the context and meaning of the words you use, telling you how prestigious your essay is and the emotional sentiment level in it.

Suggestions

Finds and highlights common writing pitfalls that could weaken your writing, displaying them in an easy-to-navigate manner.
